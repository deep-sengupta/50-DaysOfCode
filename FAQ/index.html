Frequently Asked Questions

How does the subscription service work?

Our subscription service allows you to sign up for monthly or yearly plans. You will be billed at the beginning of each billing cycle, and you can cancel anytime through your account settings.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For yearly subscriptions, we also offer invoicing upon request for business clients.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Simply go to your account settings and choose the plan that suits your needs. The changes will take effect in the next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for new users. No credit card is required during the trial period, and you can cancel anytime before the trial ends to avoid being charged.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team via email at support@example.com, or use the live chat feature available on our website. Our team is available 24/7 to assist you.
